
### IV. structure

1. passivity. 
2. negative imagination that witholds you from taking action, that might lead you to apathy, boredom, judging your possible paths as worthless. 
3. thus unprocessed disappointment with past experiences, the loop od wishing for your circumstances to be different.
4. what for?? "nothing worth getting up for, nothing worth doing, nothing worth examining".

rev.: 
1. pick the cup up: seizing the opportunity, seeing through the circumstances, finding a path out of the maze, feeling renewed excitement of it.
2. accept the challenge: unwinding your crossed arms, sitting back down at the table with whatever your hand was dealt with, renewal of interest, renewal of composure, "oh right, show me what you've got", the spark returns to your eye.
3. ace and triad switch places: you've reached your goal, but you don't feel the joy of it, "what was all that for?", is the feeling of falling behind, guilt and trying to catch up motivating your actions and thus capturing your joy of accomplishment? are you running from one thing to another? what are you running from?

### III. full expression

1. joy, celebration, harvest, good outcomes.
2. sharing the experience of life, in good and bad times as well.

rev.:
1. negation: loss of happiness, something hoped for didn't work out.
2. friendship fallout: loss or weakening of friendship, due to the distance and growing apart or being disappointed with your friends not supporting you when you needed it, though is it worth letting go of them, do you talk enough?
3. lack of friends: there are things to celebrate, but for some reasons there are no close people around you to share in the experience of joy, have you sacrificed too much for the sake of your quest for the holy grail?
